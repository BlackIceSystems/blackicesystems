About

The history behind Black Ice Systems.

  • 2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Matt Goode started Black Ice Systems in response to a need within Australian industry for a business able to assist with challenges in safety of machinery. The goal of establishing a presence as a leading service provider in functional safety, without ties to particular hardware, software, or other services.

  • March 2016

    Start of the Journey

    Our doors first opened in early 2016, working from an office in Brewer Street, Perth, Western Australia. In the first year of trading Black Ice Systems have secured work on a number of prestigious projects. Response to our services has been overwhelmingly positive, with clients impressed by our enthusiastic approach, and pragmatic solutions to their challenges.

  • December 2016

    New Premises

    In December 2016 we moved to our new location in Osborne Park, with easier access to the freeway, and additional space for our team to grow to meet the increasing in Perth need for high quality functional safety consulting services.
