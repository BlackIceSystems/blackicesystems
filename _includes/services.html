Risk Management

The risk assessment is the foundation of a project. Good risk assessments facilitate effective safety-by-design, while bad risk assessments lead to unsafe projects and increased costs. At Black Ice Systems we pride ourselves on being leaders in risk management techniques and application. Our facilitators have strong backgrounds in mining and materials handling giving us insight into the challenges encountered on large and small projects.

Functional safety can be a complicated field. Black Ice Systems can help you tackle the challenge of achieving functional safety compliance. Whether it is tailoring a full lifecycle for your business, or assistance with specific tasks on a single project. Training

Having a team with the required skills and knowledge to execute safety projects helps to avoid costly errors and delays, which can lead to deployment of unsafe systems. Carrying out effective training, at key early project stages, sets up both the project and team for success. Our suite of training courses are tailored for the functional safety challenges encountered in mining and materials handling. Black Ice Systems can provide tailored training for all levels of staff including engineers, managers, and maintainers - to ensure your team can work safely and effectively.
